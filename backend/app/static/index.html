Everything your project needs

From first invoice upload to final lender approval — Finel AI Projects handles the finance layer so you can focus on building.

AI Invoice Extraction

Upload PDFs or images. Gemini AI reads every field — vendor, invoice number, date, subtotal, HST/GST/PST, and total — in seconds. No manual data entry.

Draw & Budget Tracking

Organize every invoice into draws. Track lender-submitted amounts, approved amounts, and lender margins. See your full cost structure across categories and subdivisions.

Government Claim Management

Manage provincial and federal claims separately. Set margins, track submitted vs approved amounts, and export claim-ready reports — all linked back to the original invoices.

Categories & Vendor Mapping

Auto-classify invoices by vendor using your own category rules. Bulk re-classify in one click. Build a live cost breakdown that matches your accounting structure.

Invoice Finder

Point the tool at a folder of PDFs and it matches them against your database — finding missing invoices, duplicates, and already-processed files with live progress streaming.

Export & Reports

Export filtered invoices to CSV in one click. Configure which columns appear in exports. Share clean, accountant-ready reports without manual spreadsheet work.
